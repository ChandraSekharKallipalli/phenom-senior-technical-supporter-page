"Celebrating 5 Years of Making a Differnece"

Current employee - Exexcutive Assistantin Ambler, PA

Pros

This company provides excellent benefits for our employees. Phenom pays for the principal on my health insurance and dental plans. This adds up to significant savings each year, and the cost for the other ancillary benefit is not much. I have not heard of many companies that pay their employees for health, dental, and short-term disability plans. We are also provided with a $1,000 Mental Health reimbursement plan that can be used for the employee and their benefits, and we are reimbursed for our cell phones and home internet services each month. All of those savings add up each month. The company provides a weekly hot lunch on Wednesdays and Thursdays and many food/snack-related perks. Today we had a Kona Ice truck show up for an hour to provide free Kona Ice to employees.

Cons

The offices can be empty some days. We are on a hybrid schedule separated by departments. I miss having a large group of employees together during the day. Being together in the office promotes more open lines of communication and is suitable for my mental health to work with peers and people I call friends.
